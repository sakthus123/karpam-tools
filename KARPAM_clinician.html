CKD-EPI 2009, female, without ethnicity adjustment — the equation the model was built on. (CKD-EPI 2021 gives a slightly different value; this tool feeds 2009 to the model.)

Classify from uACR / uPCR →

Normal: uACR <3 / uPCR <15 · Moderate: uACR 3–30 / uPCR 15–50 · Severe: uACR >30 / uPCR >50. uACR takes precedence. Indian labs reporting mg/g: divide by ~8.84 for mg/mmol.

KARPAM v0.1 (Phase 0 — feasibility instrument). The risk engine reproduces the published PREDICT models (Ralston, Hamill, Santhakumaran, Hladunewich, Garg, Bramham. Kidney International Reports 2026). Used here under credit; not an original Kauvery model at this stage.

Rule-out tool. High negative predictive value (85–99%): a below-threshold result reliably reassures. Low positive predictive value (4–33%): an above-threshold result flags surveillance, not a confirmed outcome. Discrimination AUC ≈0.70.

Not validated in Indian / South Asian populations and not a registered medical device. For clinical decision support within the Kauvery nephro-obstetric MDT only. The mother module routes to care and never replaces clinical assessment.
